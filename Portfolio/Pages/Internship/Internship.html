Robotics:

Robotics is my first internship, I have actively been in my robotics club at the Academy for
Math, Engineering, and Science. We are a part of the First Robotics Competition more commonly
known as FRC. FRC is provided by First, a company aimed at preparing kids for the future and teaching
them many life skills. I am a member of AMES Robotics #3243 and we are aiming to compete in the comptition in
the begining of March. I am the Mechanical Team Lead at AMES Robotics and I'm happy to help and learn
skills in this club. I want to say that Robotics is also more that just a club. It is something that you can be doing
all year and learn many, many skills from it. To date, I have put more that 200 hours into Robotics.
