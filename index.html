Hello, I'm Zain Kergaye

Currently, I'm on a journey of learning and exploration at the University of Utah, where I'm pursuing a Bachelor's
degree in Computer Engineering, with aspirations of continuing on to earn a Master's degree in the future.

Passion for Technology

My fascination with technology has been a driving force in my life. I find joy in the world of 3D printing, Computer-Aided Design (CAD), and
coding in various languages like Java, C++, and Python. I love bringing my ideas to life through the magic of 3D printing, designing
intricate structures with CAD software, and crafting innovative solutions through coding.

Electronics Enthusiast

My interests also extend to the world of electronics and hardware. I enjoy tinkering with circuits and development
boards, exploring the vast possibilities they offer, and using them to create exciting projects. It's a constantly evolving
journey, and I'm always eager to embrace new challenges in this realm.

Community Involvement

Beyond my academic and technical pursuits, I am deeply committed to making a positive impact in my community. As a proud
member of the Utah Islamic Center Youth Committee, I actively engage in volunteer work to support and uplift those around me. My faith
and my commitment to community service are integral parts of who I am.
